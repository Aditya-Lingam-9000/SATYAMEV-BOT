How to Test the Satyamev-Bot

Follow these sandbox setup instructions to test fact-checking directly on your device.

  • 1

    Connect to the Sandbox

    Open WhatsApp and send the following activation text to the Twilio sandbox number: +1 415 523 8886.

    join explain-pain
  • 2

    Send a Claim Text

    Copy and paste any rumor or questionable news text (in English, Hindi, Telugu, etc.) to the chat.

  • 3

    Review Live Fact-Check

    Wait for the live progress bar to reach 100% and check the fully formatted verdict details returned.
